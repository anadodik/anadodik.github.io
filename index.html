Ana Dodik

· · · ·

Second-year PhD student at MIT EECS, CSAIL advised by Prof. Justin Solomon and Prof. Vincent Sitzmann. Working on problems at the intersection of graphics, vision, and algorithmic fairness.

Before MIT, I was a computer vision engineer at Meta. I have a Master's degree from ETH Zurich where I had the amazing opportunity to work with Disney Research Studios on path tracing research.
